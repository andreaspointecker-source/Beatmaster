BeatMaster

The ultimate multiplayer music quiz game testing your knowledge across decades and genres.

Application Reference: Mobile App (Android) - Music Quiz Game using Apple Music API for previews and driving traffic/subscriptions to Apple Music.

How It Works

Choose your Challenge

Filter by genres, decades, or origin to match your music taste.

Play with Friends

Connect with a room code or host a live hotseat multiplayer session directly on your phone.

Guess the Track

Listen to a 30-second Apple Music preview and guess the Title, Artist, or Year!

Discover & Stream

Learn the facts, view high-quality covers, and directly open the song on Apple Music to stream it fully!
