BeatMaster

The ultimate multiplayer music quiz game testing your knowledge across decades and genres. Built with the Apple Music API.

Application Context / Reviewer Notice "Mobile App (Android) - Music Quiz Game using Apple Music API for previews and driving traffic/subscriptions to Apple Music."
Step 1 — Setup

Unlock & Choose Your Category

Players start their journey by browsing through a rich catalog of music decades, specific genres, and special themed playlists like movie soundtracks.

To keep players engaged and add progression, they have to unlock new playlists. Once selected, you can customize the game rules, round amounts, and difficulty before starting your session.

Step 2 — Multiplayer Lobby

Invite Your Friends

BeatMaster is meant to be played together! Host a live online room natively in the app. Your friends can easily join by entering your room code or by scanning a QR code.

The host controls the game flow, starting the music round and navigating through questions, ensuring everyone is synced in real-time.

Step 3 — Gameplay

Listen & Guess the Track

The core gameplay relies on high-quality 30-second audio previews powered directly by the Apple Music API.

While the virtual vinyl record spins and the track plays seamlessly in the background, players have a limited time to recognize the song. The game dynamically asks different questions: Sometimes you need to name the artist, sometimes the exact year or the song title itself.

Step 4 — Resolution & Conversion

Discover & Stream on Apple Music

After the song stops, the resolution screen reveals the correct answer alongside beautiful album cover art fetched from iTunes.

Here, players can see the exact metadata of the track. If they loved the song, a highly prominent "Listen on Apple Music" button is placed directly below the album art. This serves as a powerful call-to-action, directly referring highly engaged and curious users straight into the Apple Music ecosystem as potential subscribers.
